Devanagari Character Recognition

By: Sagar 930 264

A Devanagari Character Recognition System utilizes machine learning algorithms to accurately identify and interpret characters written in the Devanagari script, which is commonly used for languages like Hindi and Nepali. It enables tasks such as optical character recognition (OCR), converting printed or handwritten Devanagari text into digital formats. This system facilitates text processing, translation, and information retrieval, contributing to the digitization and accessibility of Devanagari-script content across digital platforms while preserving cultural heritage.

Image Colorization

By: Sagar 674 184

Image colorization with GANs uses two neural networks: a generator and a discriminator. The generator creates colorized versions of grayscale images, while the discriminator distinguishes between real color images and fake colorizations. During training, they compete to improve the generator's ability to produce realistic colorizations. Once trained, the generator can colorize grayscale images accurately, offering a powerful tool for enhancing and restoring visual content.
